AI 101 (in human language)

AI is a big umbrella. Under it: Machine Learning and Deep Learning. Generative AI is the part that creates new content (text, images, audio, video, code).

  • Generative AI = next-word prediction (a.k.a. ‚Äúsmart autocomplete‚Äù).
  • It turns words into tokens (little chunks), then predicts what token should come next.
  • Different training data ‚Üí different strengths, weaknesses, and ‚Äúvibes.‚Äù

TL;DR: You don‚Äôt ‚Äúsearch‚Äù AI. You collaborate with it. The quality comes from your context + your follow-ups.

Key risks (and how to not get dunked on)

AI is useful‚Ä¶ and it‚Äôs also confident, biased, and occasionally a lovable liar. Here are the big ones:

  • ‚ÄúAI slop / workslop‚Äù ‚Äî high-volume content that‚Äôs unverified and low-signal.

  • Use a ‚Äúsafe word‚Äù with family/friends to confirm identity in weird situations.
